What is Black Eye Bean?


It's a shopping tool with access to groceries around their peak, for less. Delivery is our core service. And, we bundle ingredients by recipe for quick meal ideas. Buying food at it's peak is great. Here's why:

  • You get fresh food and save money. Stores want to sell peak food quickly so it doesn't start to show signs of aging (marketing thing). Black Eye Bean helps clear that inventory and gets you a discount.

  • You have to use your groceries soon. Food often gets thrown away after waiting too long to be used. Buying food to make soon will avoid that waste.

  • You're making a statement about avoiding food waste. Encourage better practices across the industry with your purchasing power.
